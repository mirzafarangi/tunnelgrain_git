Service Policy

Clear terms for our VPN service usage and support

Payment & Refund Policy

No Refunds Policy

All sales are final. We do not offer refunds for VPN purchases except in cases of proven technical failure on our end.

Why No Refunds?
  • We provide a free 15-minute test before purchase
  • You can verify compatibility and performance before buying
  • This keeps our service affordable and sustainable
Technical Failure Exception

Refunds may be considered if:

  • Our servers are down for more than 48 consecutive hours
  • Your VPN config is technically faulty due to our error
  • Service is completely inaccessible due to our infrastructure failure

Technical failures must be documented and verified by our support team.

Service Terms

Access Duration
  • Monthly VPN: 30 days from purchase date
  • Test VPN: 15 minutes from activation
  • No extensions or rollover of unused time
Fair Usage
  • Service is for legitimate personal use only
  • No commercial reselling or sharing of credentials
  • No illegal activities or network abuse
  • We reserve the right to terminate service for violations
Technical Limitations
  • Limited to 10 concurrent monthly users
  • Service availability depends on server status
  • Performance may vary based on location and network conditions
  • We provide service "as-is" without performance guarantees

Technical Support

What We Help With
  • WireGuard configuration assistance
  • Connection troubleshooting
  • Config file re-delivery
  • Server status inquiries
Response Times

Support requests are handled on a best-effort basis. Response time typically 24-48 hours via email.

Contact Methods
  • Email: support@tunnelgrain.com
  • Include your purchase details and detailed description of the issue

Privacy Practices

What We Don't Log
  • Your browsing activity or destinations
  • Connection timestamps or duration
  • Data transferred or bandwidth usage
  • DNS requests or IP addresses visited
What We Do Track

  • Payment records for billing purposes
  • Basic server performance metrics
  • Support ticket history
