VPN speed can be affected by several factors:

  • Distance: Your physical distance from our server affects speed
  • ISP throttling: Some ISPs may slow down VPN traffic
  • Network congestion: Try connecting at different times
  • Device performance: Older devices may process encryption slower

If speeds are consistently very slow, contact support with your location details.
